We are a family owned an operated business. We first opened our doors more than 30 years ago.

Primo Toscano opened pizza restaurant "Pizza Bubonja's" in Comuna of Pontevico. His grandmother was famous around comuna from her exquisite cooking. This pizza receipts became a sacred tradition in the Primo family and a legend in the Comuna of Pontevico in the Province of Brescian, in Lombardy.

Today we are the most known pizza restaurant in Italy with a big tradition and people come here from all sides of Italy for the pizzas.

From our modest beginnings, we are growing to become the famous pizza restaurant in Italy. Pizza Bubonja's has been acclaimed "Italian's best pizza" by RAI, Sky Italia, Corriere della Sera, la Republica, il Giornale and more!

If you love pizza, come here and enjoy one of the best pizza experiences ever!!!
